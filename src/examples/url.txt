title: URL
---
URI     = (scheme ':')? ('//' auth)?
          path ('?' query)? ('#' frag)?
scheme  = ~[:/?#]+
auth    = ~[/?#]*
path    = ~[?#]*
query   = ~'#'*
frag    = ~[ \t\n\r]*
---
http://www.ics.uci.edu/pub/ietf/uri/#Related
---
Equivalent to the regular expression for
well-formed URI's in RFC 3986.
